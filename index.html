Torah Leadership

The parsha teaches about the king of Israel — he must not collect too much wealth or horses, and he must always carry a Torah with him. The message is that true leadership isn't about power or glory, but about humility and staying connected to Hashem's values.

✨ Takeaway: Real strength comes from living with Torah values and remembering that even leaders are servants of something greater.
